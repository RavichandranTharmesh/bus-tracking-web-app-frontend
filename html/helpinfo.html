How the Bus Tracking System Works

Our system provides real-time GPS tracking for buses. You can find your bus's current location, view estimated arrival times, and get route information. Use the app to plan your trip and reduce waiting times at bus stops.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you’re facing issues with the web app, try the following steps:

  • GPS not updating: Ensure your phone's location services are enabled, and you have a stable internet connection.
  • Bus not appearing: If the bus you’re tracking doesn’t show, it may be due to a technical issue or the bus may be off-route. Contact customer support if the issue persists.
  • Slow app performance: Try closing other function running in the background.

web App Usage Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to using our bus tracking system:

  1. Enter your bus number in the search bar.
  2. View the real-time location and the estimated arrival time.
  3. Track the number of stops remaining before your destination.
  4. Enable push notifications to receive alerts when the bus is approaching your stop.
